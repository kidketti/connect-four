How to Play

  • Description:
  • Connect Four is a game in which two players are asigned tokens/markers of different color. These token/marker colors may vary from black and red, or yellow and red. The objective of the game is to be the first player to line up four of the same color markers in a row on the playboard. The player able to win in the following directions: vertically, horizontally, and/or diagonally.
  • How to Play:
    1. Decide who wants to go first (Player 1). This player is assigned the red token/marker. The second player (Player 2) is assigned the yellow token/marker.
    2. The player's turns are alternated. After a column is selected, that player will "drop" their piece to the available slot of that column.
    3. Continue until one player wins, or the board is completely filled and there is a tie.
